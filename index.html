This dual-action, characterized by one diaphragm filling as the other empties, allows the pump to maintain a steady flow of fluid. AODD pumps are adept at handling a wide array of fluids, from thin and watery to highly viscous or abrasive, effectively minimizing the risk of damage to the pump.

Key to their efficiency and reliability are the pump’s stationary seals, which drastically reduce leakage risks and enable the pump to run dry without damage. What makes Burks Pumps unique compared to other brands?

Burks Pumps are designed with advanced features like self-adjusting mechanical seals, cast in vortex pockets, and external impeller adjustments, making them more efficient and easier to maintain. Their pumps also offer a broad range of configurations to suit different industrial needs.

Can Burks Pumps handle high-pressure applications?

Yes, Burks Pumps, especially the regenerative turbine pumps, are designed to handle high-pressure applications effectively. They are built to ensure high performance and long service life even under demanding conditions.

Are Burks Pumps suitable for handling hot water?

Absolutely. Burks Pumps are engineered to manage high-temperature fluids, making them ideal for boiler feed, hot water circulation, and similar applications.

How easy is it to maintain Burks Pumps?

Burks Pumps are designed with maintenance in mind. Features like the external impeller adjustment and easy disassembly make routine maintenance straightforward, reducing downtime and extending the pump’s lifespan.

What materials are Burks Pumps made from?

Burks Pumps are constructed from high-quality materials such as bronze, cast iron, and stainless steel, ensuring durability and resistance to corrosion.
